This Online Photography Course is a complete and comprehensive program in Digital Photography.
I'm Jeff Roush, a photographer who has worked in the professional photographic field for over 40 years.
Along with running a successful studios around the USA I have taught photography at the college level for many years.
I have come to know the difficulty in learning this craft through many face to face encounters with students through the years.
In developing this program I've strived to make it simple to follow, complete, and very affordable.

This is a real course, similar to what you might experience if you took a credited college program from a major university.
It's not a "watered-down" imitation that you may have already seen or tried. In this course you'll learn photography;
in fact, it mirrors the college courses that I presently teach at two Universities in Tennessee.
We'll cover a lot of material and you'll learn it all, regardless of the time or effort it takes.
Together we discuss all the basics and a lot of advanced techniques used by pros to make their job simpler,
more consistent, and easier to remember.

I urge you to look around on this site, view some images done by students (coming soon), review my information and credentials,
and look through the syllabus to see what you might learn.
If you're serious about learning photography, or have a dream to open your own studio,
this is the course for you. Click on "course overview" on the left and learn more about this great program.

Questions? jroush@roushstudios.com for an answer
