About the Instructor/Photographer

by Doug Wong


Jeff Roush is a full-time commercial photographer and university photography instructor.
He started his first studio in Toledo, Ohio in 1983. He gained many successes with Fortune 500 companies in Toldeo and other major markets very quickly.
By 1989 he was ready to move on to even bigger challenges, so he packed up and moved to Los Angeles where he started the LA clone of Roush Studios.
He chose to return to a slightly slower lifestyle and moved to western Tennessee, where has a studio,
teaches at two community colleges and still travels a good amount of time shooting commercial photography for clients around the USA.
Jeff also has a workshop company and an online photography course series.
He has also been the Education Director for the CCCT for the past few years.
You will enjoy his "easy to understand" lecturing and instuction.
