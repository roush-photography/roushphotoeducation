Camera Modes

We'll discuss in detail all of the automatic modes of the modern day digital camera;

focusing moslty on the "A" (Nikon), "Av" (Canon)and the "S" (Nikon), "Tv" (Canon) modes.

These are two modes that escape most photographers.

We'll also discuss how to use the ISO to make your photographic challenges easier to solve.

Many photographers lack complete knowledge of these photographic settings.

This workshop will help you totally understand them .... and will make your images a lot better very quickly.

Outdoor & Indoor Portraiture

We will discuss in great detail shooting portrature in available light, both indoors and outdoors.

This part of the business on one of the most lucrative for young photographers trying to break into the business..

It's a part of the business that's easy to get and easy to do if you have all the information about doing it properly.

This will help you with your portraiture work, your wedding work, and your event work; not to mention helping with

shooting in general. Some of the simplist photographic endeavors can seem so difficult.

Indoor & Outdoor Speedlight Portraiture

 

This portion of our workshop will focus on using a speedlight (camera flash). If you already have one then you probably hate it.

I haven't met too many photographers that know how to use one effectively in all photographic situations.

We'll be discussing and usingsome inexpensive photography gear that makes using this type light source

very easy and very predictable. You don't need a speedlight to attend this workshop.
